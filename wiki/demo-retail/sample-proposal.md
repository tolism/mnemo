# AI Integration Proposal for RetailCorp

## Executive Summary
RetailCorp seeks to implement AI-powered inventory management across 50 stores in the GCC region. Budget: $500K. Timeline: 6 months.

## Current Challenges
- Manual inventory counts take 40 hours per store per month
- Stockout rate of 8% causes $2M annual revenue loss
- No demand forecasting capability

## Proposed Solution
Deploy computer vision cameras at shelf level with real-time stock monitoring. ML model predicts demand 7 days ahead with 92% accuracy based on historical POS data, weather, and local events.

## Team
- Project Lead: Sarah Chen (15 years retail tech)
- ML Engineer: Raj Patel (ex-Amazon forecasting team)
- Implementation: 3 field engineers
