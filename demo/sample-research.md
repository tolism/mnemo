# GCC Retail AI Market Analysis

## Market Size
The GCC retail technology market reached $3.2B in 2025, growing at 18% CAGR. AI-specific solutions account for approximately $400M, projected to reach $1.1B by 2028.

| Standard AI | Autonomous retail | San Francisco | $235M | None |

## UAE-Specific Factors
- Dubai Retail Strategy 2025 targets 25% digital integration in retail
- DIFC and ADGM offer regulatory sandboxes for AI testing
- Labor cost inflation driving automation demand (15% increase 2024-2025)
- UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) effective Jan 2026 affects data collection

## Opportunity Assessment
The gap: no dominant AI inventory management player in GCC. Trax has presence but focuses on CPG brands, not retailers directly. Focal Systems is US-focused. The market is open for a GCC-native solution.
